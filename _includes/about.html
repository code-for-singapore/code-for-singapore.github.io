About Us

We are part of the global network Code for All which is an international network of civic tech organizations that drive change through digital technology, citizen participation, collaborative decision-making and good governance to deliver solutions for social challenges while improving the relationship between governments and citizens.

Code for Singapore believes that digital technology can empower non-profits and be used to solve the gaps in society.

Code for Singapore's Mission

To empower non-profits with the necessary technology and deliver solutions for social change through the lens of technology.

Our Vision

Technology for social good
