Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Will this be done on a website?

Yes for now we are building a website to manage this, but it could evolve to an app in future.


How will getting seniors to share their experience help with their social isolation?

Studies show what benefits of contributing back, or volunteering has on the mental and emotional wellness of the elderly, or for that matter for any age group. We hope to solve social isolation by giving the elderly a chance to share what they have most which is experience.


Is this service free of charge?

Yes we intend this service to be free for all.


Who is running this?

Majority of us are working adults from various industries but mostly IT volunteering our time and skills in this project because we believe that Tech is an enabler for the good of society.
