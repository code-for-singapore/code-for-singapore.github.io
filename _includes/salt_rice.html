Salt Rice

A continuance of Code For Singapore - Elderly in Singapore workshop series conducted by ThinkPlace's Bill Bannear and gelled together by Pei-Chun Ko and her team.

Salt Rice's Mission

Reducing social isolation in elderly by giving them the opportunity to share their life lessons with the community.


The Problem

In 2015, more than 1 in 2 Singaporeans aged 60 years and above reported being lonely

Even among those living with families, 1 in 5 elderly above 75 in Singapore, exhibit signs of depression
